Deadpool aka "The Merc with a Mouth"

Deadpool or real name Wade Winston Wilson is something of an anti-hero and a mercenary created by Rob Liefeld and Fabian Nicieza that is know for being a excellent marksman he has the mutant ability of accelerated healing factor which was given to him after undergoing the Weapon X program this enables him to regenerate any destroyed tissue at a super-human rate as well as making him immune to all known diseases. Because he had cancerous tumors he was suffering from at the time of the Weapon X, program causing them to quickly spread across his entire body. Because of this, his healing factor accelerated his cancer, resulting in massive scars causing his appearance to be severely disfigured and one of the reason for the complete body suite.Because of his big mouth and flashy attitude he has been giving the nickname"Merc with a Mouth".

What is Deadpool Alliance?

The Deadpool Alliance is a fan-site dedicated to the Marvel Comics anti-hero character Deadpool. Here we offer an outlet that allows fan to express themselves and share their devotion with other fans across the globe.
